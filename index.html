DEAL

Post Generator

Paste any shopping link (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, short/affiliate links…) → get a merged image + title.

Works fully in your browser; images are merged client-side, nothing is stored anywhere.
One link → single-product mode (all its images merged). Two or more links → collage mode (first image of each, up to 8 products).
On Android Chrome, use Share to WhatsApp for a true one-tap post. On desktop/iPhone, use Copy + paste.
